About Restroom Finder

Restroom Finder is a dedicated platform for individuals with disabilities to locate accessible restrooms with ease and dignity.

We understand the challenges faced by disabled people in finding suitable restroom facilities while traveling, shopping, or simply being out in public. Our mission is to empower the community by providing up-to-date, reliable information about accessible restroom locations across the country.

Our Mission

To create a comprehensive and user-friendly resource that helps individuals with disabilities find safe, accessible restrooms in any location.

Our Vision

A world where accessibility is not an afterthought, and everyone can find the amenities they need without undue stress or difficulty.

Our Values

  • Accessibility for all
  • Community-driven support
  • Up-to-date and accurate information
  • Privacy and dignity for our users
